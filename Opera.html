Operating Principles

Soosooland Educational Games Platform

Operating Principles Privacy Policy Contact & Support

1. Copyright & Ownership

  • Games must be created and owned by the submitting creators with full legal rights to distribute.
  • Copyright remains with the creator; Soosooland serves only as a linking and showcase platform.
  • Unauthorized uploads are strictly prohibited; creators bear full legal responsibility for their submissions.
  • Creators may request removal of their games from Soosooland at any time with 24-hour processing guarantee.
  • If Analytics/AdSense codes are included, all data and revenue remain with the creator's accounts.
  • Soosooland disclaims all liability for operation, revenue, legal issues, or content of submitted games.
  • Dispute Resolution: Copyright disputes will be handled according to DMCA procedures. Contact soosooland2025@gmail.com for issues.

2. Technical Requirements & Standards

  • Games must run in modern web browsers (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+) without requiring installation.
  • Mobile-responsive design is mandatory; games must function properly on devices with screen widths from 320px to 1920px.
  • Games may be hosted on GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify, itch.io, or similar platforms; Soosooland links to the provided URL.
  • Loading time should not exceed 10 seconds on standard broadband connections.
  • Total game size should not exceed 50MB for optimal performance.
  • Demo versions are allowed but must be clearly labeled as "Demo" or "Trial Version".
  • Games must pass basic security screening; vulnerable code or malicious content will result in immediate rejection.
  • HTTPS hosting is required for all submitted games.

3. Content Quality & Educational Standards

  • Primary focus: educational, brain-training, puzzle, strategy, and skill-development games.
  • Each game must provide clear learning objectives, educational value statement, or cognitive benefits.
  • Age appropriateness must be clearly stated (e.g., "Ages 6-12", "Teen+", "All Ages").
  • Prohibited content: violence, discrimination, hate speech, sexually explicit material, gambling mechanics, or substance abuse themes.
  • Games promoting critical thinking, problem-solving, creativity, or academic subjects are prioritized.
  • Submissions failing educational value, safety, or appropriateness standards will be rejected with detailed feedback.
  • Accessibility features (keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility) are encouraged and may receive priority placement.

Submission Review Process

  • Day 1-2: Initial technical and content screening
  • Day 3-5: Educational value assessment and testing
  • Day 6-7: Final review and decision notification
  • Maximum processing time: 7 business days

4. Advertisements & External Content Policy

  • Even if the game meets our standards, inappropriate advertisements or linked content may result in restrictions.
  • Prohibited advertising content: gambling, adult/mature content, illegal substances, copyright-infringing materials, political campaigns, or misleading claims.
  • All advertisements and external links undergo pre-approval review.
  • Creators must disclose all third-party services, analytics, and advertising networks used.
  • Changes to advertising content must be reported within 48 hours of implementation.
  • Child-directed games (under 13) must comply with COPPA advertising restrictions.

5. Operation & Maintenance Responsibilities

  • Creators are fully responsible for ongoing maintenance, updates, and bug fixes.
  • User reports and technical issues must be addressed within 7 business days.
  • Major updates or planned maintenance must be communicated to Soosooland 48 hours in advance.
  • Games offline for more than 30 days without notice may be temporarily delisted.
  • Soosooland reserves the right to remove inappropriate links immediately without prior notice (original hosting remains unaffected).
  • Creators must maintain updated contact information for platform communications.

6. Privacy Protection & Minor Safety

  • Soosooland does not directly collect personal information; we only provide links to creators' games.
  • Creators must comply with GDPR, COPPA, and applicable privacy laws in their jurisdictions.
  • Games targeting children under 14 must not collect personal information without verifiable parental consent.
  • All games must include clear privacy policies if any data collection occurs.
  • Cookie usage, analytics tracking, and third-party services must be disclosed to users.
  • Reports of improper data practices will result in immediate investigation and potential removal.
  • Child safety is our top priority; any content potentially harmful to minors will be immediately removed.

  • 무단 업로드는 엄격히 금지되며, 제작자는 제출물에 대한 모든 법적 책임을 집니다.

  • 수수랜드는 제출 게임의 운영, 수익, 법적 문제 또는 콘텐츠에 대해 일체 책임을 지지 않습니다.

2. 기술적 요구사항 및 표준

  • 게임은 최신 웹브라우저(Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+)에서 별도 설치 없이 실행되어야 합니다.
  • 모바일 반응형 디자인은 필수이며, 320px~1920px 화면 너비에서 정상 작동해야 합니다.
  • GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify, itch.io 등의 플랫폼 호스팅 가능하며, 수수랜드는 제공된 URL로 연결합니다.
  • 표준 광대역 연결에서 로딩 시간은 10초를 초과하지 않아야 합니다.
  • 게임 전체 용량은 최적 성능을 위해 50MB를 초과하지 않아야 합니다.
  • 데모 버전 등록 가능하나 "데모" 또는 "체험판"으로 명확히 표시해야 합니다.
  • 기본 보안 검사를 통과해야 하며, 취약한 코드나 악성 콘텐츠 발견 시 즉시 거부됩니다.
  • 모든 제출 게임은 HTTPS 호스팅이 필수입니다.

3. 콘텐츠 품질 및 교육적 기준

  • 주요 초점: 교육, 두뇌 개발, 퍼즐, 전략, 기능 개발 게임
  • 각 게임은 명확한 학습 목표, 교육적 가치 설명, 또는 인지적 이점을 제시해야 합니다.
  • 연령 적합성을 명확히 표시해야 합니다(예: "6-12세", "청소년 이상", "전연령").
  • 금지 콘텐츠: 폭력, 차별, 혐오 발언, 성적 노골적 내용, 도박 요소, 약물 남용 주제
  • 비판적 사고, 문제 해결, 창의성, 학문 과목을 촉진하는 게임이 우선됩니다.
  • 교육적 가치, 안전성, 적합성 기준 미달 시 상세한 피드백과 함께 거부됩니다.
  • 접근성 기능(키보드 탐색, 스크린 리더 호환성)이 권장되며 우선 배치될 수 있습니다.

제출 검토 프로세스

  • 1-2일차: 초기 기술 및 콘텐츠 심사
  • 3-5일차: 교육적 가치 평가 및 테스트
  • 6-7일차: 최종 검토 및 결정 통보
  • 최대 처리 시간: 영업일 기준 7일
